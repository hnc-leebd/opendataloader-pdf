# Issue Policy

## Type Labels
(choose ONE OR MORE from the list below):

- type/bug - Bug report
- type/enhancement - Feature request or improvement
- type/docs - Documentation
- type/dependencies - Dependency update

## Priority Labels
(choose exactly ONE):

- priority/P0: Highest priority; production blocking, severe regression, data loss.
- priority/P1: Important but not immediately blocking.
- priority/P2: Normal or low priority.

## Estimated
(Story points — choose exactly ONE):

- 1, 2, 3, 5, 8

### Definitions:

- 1 → Very simple, trivial work. (less than 1 day)
- 2 → Small task, low complexity, straightforward. (1–2 days)
- 3 → Medium task, average story size (3–5 days).
- 5 → Large task, multiple steps and considerations. (6–10 days)
- 8 → Very large task, complex with higher uncertainty. (more than 10 days)
